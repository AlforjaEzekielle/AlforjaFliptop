FlipTop Battle League is the Philippines' premier rap battle platform, established in 2010. It has grown to become an iconic scene where MCs showcase their lyrical skills, creativity, and wit in intense verbal battles. The "Isabuhay" tournament is FlipTop's annual, most prestigious competition, crowning the top battle MC each year.
